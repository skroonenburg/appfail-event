About Us

Appfail is a labor of love from two devs Sam & Peter. Read more about the co-founders below.
 
 

Sam

Sam loves coding. If he's not at work coding, he's at home coding. If he's not coding at all, he's thinking about it. Sam likes solving complex problems, that's why he's a software development lead at Kiandra IT.

Before this he was a developer for Microsoft working on Windows Vista, Windows 7 and FAST Search. On the Windows team he rewrote the Disk Defragmenter for Windows 7 and loved every second of it.

Sam loved Microsoft's internal exception monitoring system and wanted to bring the best of this to ASP.NET -- and hence, Appfail was born. Follow @samkroon on twitter or send him an email.

Peter

Peter digs when things run smoothly and according to plan. This gives him time to read hacker news and follow tweets from @codinghorror, @ayende and many others.

One day, however, Peter felt that things could be done even better so he joined Sam in creating Appfail.

Peter works primarily with .NET/C# but also enjoys dabbling with other languages and frameworks. He holds a PhD in Computer Science from Monash University and has worked across government and the private sector.

At the moment Peter is conjuring some very cool apps at Kiandra IT. Don't be a stranger - send him an email or follow @sbarski on twitter.
